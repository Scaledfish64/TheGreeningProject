Solar Energy

One very popular program is installing and utilizing solar energy in electricity systems around a household. Solar energy is clean, and obtained directly from the Sun. It is a sustainable and renewable energy source which can work off the grid . Homeowners can install solar panels on their property in order to generate electricity, or participate in community programs to reduce the cost. Community programs allow homeowners to pay a monthly fee on a shared solar panel array, but they receive rebates for the amount of electricity that the panels they were paying for generated.
